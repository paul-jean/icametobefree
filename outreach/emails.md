# Outreach drafts

Starting points, not finished text — they're written in a plain, direct voice
with no pitch varnish, which is the right register for all of these. Change
anything that doesn't sound like you. The one rule: **don't make them longer.**

Everything here is you contacting them. Nothing in this file responds to an
inbound offer, because inbound offers to FriesenPress authors are fraud by
default (see CAMPAIGN.md).

**Their guidelines, verified from the form itself:**

## 7. Instagram — the weekly post

Not a template, because a template will read like one. The shape that works:

- **The card is the post.** Download the square from the site.
- **The caption is where that passage came from.** The moment, the feeling, what you were working through. Two to four sentences.
- **No call to action** three weeks out of four. Book link lives in your bio.
- **Once a month:** "This is from my book, *I came to be free* — link in bio."

Example, for "Forgive yourself / For being the fish / Who forgot about water":

> This one took me years to understand, let alone write. You can be surrounded
> by the thing you're desperate for and not see it, because you've never known
> anything else. The forgiving part isn't for the big failures. It's for the
> years you spent not noticing.

That's the register: specific, unguarded about the feeling, private about the
facts. Exactly the line you said you wanted to walk.
