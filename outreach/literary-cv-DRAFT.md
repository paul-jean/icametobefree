## Notes for the rebuild

- **Don't pad.** Their evaluators read many of these. A short honest CV reads as an emerging poet; a padded one reads as someone who doesn't know the difference between a credential and a hobby. "Self-published on my website" or "posted on Instagram" belong nowhere on this page.
- **The Art of Autism feature is real and should stay** — it's a genuine editorial selection by a third party with a substantial readership. Just don't describe it as a literary journal, because it isn't, and they'll know.
- **The website is worth mentioning nowhere on the CV** — but it *does* help you under the Full-membership "findability" criterion (30% of that evaluation), which isn't the one you're applying under. Save it.
- **If the honest answer to Performance / Community / Education / Awards is "nothing yet,"** that's a real answer. It means the application rests on two self-published collections plus the AoA feature, under the additional-criteria route — and it means Poetry Pause first is the better play.
